  • Tamanu oil

    Tamanu Oil possesses the unique process of cicatrisation, which means the oil has the capacity to encourage the generation of new tissue, accelerate wound healing and stimulate the growth of healthy skin cells. Giving every customer every advantage to grow new, strong hair.

  • Marula oil

    Marula oil can help nourish hair from root to tip, without making it greasy. The oil has hydrating, moisturizing, and occlusive (preventing water loss) properties, making it beneficial for dry, frizzy, or brittle hair.

This is a VERY important ingredient in the Thiicky range.

  • Jojoba oil

    Jojoba oil is added to hair conditioners to give you added protection against dryness, breakage, and split ends. The oil can also moisturize the scalp and may be a dandruff remedy. Jojoba is also rich in vitamins and minerals that nourish hair, including vitamin C, B vitamins, vitamin E, copper, and zinc.

What is Tamanu Oil?

Tamanu Oil is an amazing oil sourced from the nut off a Tamanu tree.

Tamanu Oil is full of antioxidants, moisturisers and good fatty acids. This wonder oil has many benefits for hair, ranging from scalp and skin care at the roots, to hydrating and repairing the very tips of any damaged follicle.

Extensive research has been done into this wonderful nut full of liquid gold... So we put it in a shampoo range, just for you!

Why do you suggest to shampoo twice?

Your first shampoo is responsible for removing oils and build-up on your scalp. It is ALSO responsible for opening up your cuticle layer (your hair shaft). This allows the second shampoo to CLEAN the hair shaft itself, leaving you with weightless hair, giving you better styles for a longer period of time.
