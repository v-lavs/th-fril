Main INGREDIENTS

  • Tamanu oil

    Tamanu Oil possesses the unique process of cicatrisation, which means the oil has the capacity to encourage the generation of new tissue, accelerate wound healing and stimulate the growth of healthy skin cells. Giving every customer every advantage to grow new, strong hair.

  • Marula oil

    Marula oil can help nourish hair from root to tip, without making it greasy. The oil has hydrating, moisturizing, and occlusive (preventing water loss) properties, making it beneficial for dry, frizzy, or brittle hair.

  • Seaweed extract

    New research shows seaweed extract may help control oiliness on the scalp, a new study in the journal Archives of Plastic Surgery suggests that a mix of two seaweed extracts stimulates hair-growth-promoting hormones in as little as two weeks. This is a VERY important ingredient in the Thiicky range.

  • Jojoba oil

    Jojoba oil is added to hair conditioners to give you added protection against dryness, breakage, and split ends. The oil can also moisturize the scalp and may be a dandruff remedy. Jojoba is also rich in vitamins and minerals that nourish hair, including vitamin C, B vitamins, vitamin E, copper, and zinc.
